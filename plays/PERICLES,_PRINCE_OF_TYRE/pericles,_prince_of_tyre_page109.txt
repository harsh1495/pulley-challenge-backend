FIRST GENTLEMAN.
But to have divinity preached there! did you ever dream of such a
thing?

SECOND GENTLEMAN.
No, no. Come, I am for no more bawdy houses: shall's go hear the

FIRST GENTLEMAN.
I'll do anything now that is virtuous; but I am out of the road of
rutting for ever.

 [_Exeunt._]
